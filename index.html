❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

How do I book an appointment?
+
You can book an appointment by filling out the form on this page and it will be sent directly via WhatsApp. You can also call us at +91 99999 99999 or walk in during clinic hours (Mon–Sat 9AM–8PM).
Is root canal treatment painful?
+
No! With modern anaesthesia and rotary techniques, root canal treatment at our clinic is virtually painless. Most patients are surprised at how comfortable the experience is. You will feel pressure but not pain.
What are the clinic timings?
+
Our clinic is open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. On Sundays we are open from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM for consultations. We recommend calling ahead to confirm availability for Sunday slots.
Do you treat children at this clinic?
+
Absolutely! We welcome patients of all ages including children. Our team is specially trained to make young patients feel safe, relaxed, and comfortable throughout their dental or ENT treatment.
What happens during my first ENT consultation?
+
During your first ENT consultation, Dr. Rekha will review your medical history, discuss your symptoms, and conduct a thorough physical examination of your ears, nose, and throat. A tailored treatment plan will then be discussed with you.
Are your instruments sterilized between patients?
+
Yes, absolutely. We follow strict sterilisation protocols using autoclaved instruments for every patient. Single-use disposables are used wherever possible to guarantee the highest level of infection control and patient safety.
How much does a dental implant cost?
+
Dental implant costs vary based on the number of implants, bone condition, and crown type required. We offer a free initial consultation where Dr. Rekha will assess your case and provide a transparent, detailed treatment estimate.
